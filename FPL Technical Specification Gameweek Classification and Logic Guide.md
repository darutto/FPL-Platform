Gameweek Classification Logic1.1 DefinitionsNormal Gameweek: Exactly 10 fixtures; all 20 teams play exactly once.Blank Gameweek (BGW): Fewer than 10 fixtures; one or more teams have zero fixtures.Double Gameweek (DGW): More than 10 total fixtures; one or more teams play twice within the same scoring window.1.2 Deterministic Triggers for PostponementsFixtures are postponed and relocated based on the following cup clashes:Carabao Cup Final: Clashes with GW31. Finalists and their opponents blank.FA Cup Quarter-Finals: Played April 4–5. Does not clash with league games but determines future blanks.FA Cup Semi-Finals: Clashes with GW34. Winners of the Quarter-Finals and their GW34 opponents blank.FA Cup Final: Clashes with GW37. Finalists and their opponents may blank.1.3 Logic for Rescheduling (DGW Creation)Postponed fixtures are typically moved to midweeks without European (UCL/UEL/UECL) matches.Primary Landing Zones: GW33 and GW36.The "European Status" Variable:If a team (e.g., Arsenal, Liverpool) is active in European Quarter-Finals/Semi-Finals, they lack midweek space to move a BGW34 game into the same week. This forces the DGW into later weeks like GW36.If a team (e.g., Chelsea, West Ham) is out of Europe, a postponed GW34 match can technically be played in the midweek of GW34 itself, potentially avoiding a "blank" for FPL purposes.2. 2025/26 Season Master ScheduleGameweekClassificationPrimary CauseImpacted Teams (High Probability)GW31BGW (Confirmed)Carabao Cup FinalArsenal, Man City, Wolves, Crystal Palace.GW33DGW (Likely)GW31/GW34 relocationsUp to 8 teams (e.g., Arsenal, Chelsea, Man City).GW34BGW (Likely)FA Cup Semi-FinalsTeams winning QFs (Apr 4-5).GW36DGW (Likely)GW34 relocationsTeams in Europe during GW34.GW37BGW (Potential)FA Cup FinalFA Cup finalists (e.g., Liverpool, Aston Villa).3. Scoring Logic & Rules Updates3.1 Defensive Contribution (DEFCON) PointsOutfield players earn a fixed +2 point bonus (capped) for defensive actions.Defenders (CBIT):$\text{Threshold} \geq 10$ actions.$\text{Actions} = \text{Clearances} + \text{Blocks} + \text{Interceptions} + \text{Tackles}$.Midfielders/Forwards (CBIRT):$\text{Threshold} \geq 12$ actions.$\text{Actions} = \text{Clearances} + \text{Blocks} + \text{Interceptions} + \text{Tackles} + \text{Ball Recoveries}$.3.2 Effective Ownership (EO) Calculation$EO$ is used to assess rank risk. A player's $EO$ can exceed 100% due to captaincy.Formula: $EO = \frac{\text{Started} + \text{Captained} + \text{Triple Captained}}{\text{Total Managers}}$.Advice Logic:Leading: Match $EO$ of rivals (Shield strategy).Chasing: Attack low $EO$ players (Sword strategy).4.
